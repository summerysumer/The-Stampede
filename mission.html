Our Mission

The Stampede is a student-run newspaper and club at Rancho Bernardo High School. The Stampede covers school events, achievements, and perspective, as well as views and opinions beyond the high school. We work to promote journalism, presentation, reading, and writing amongst students. Go Broncos!
